Welcome to #DANCEMAP. Though powerful search engines allow us to get a feel for certain predominant ideas about dance, the global diversity of perspectives about what "dance" means is often lost or difficult to grasp. This website is designed to provide a lens into what people around the world are "saying" about dance in text, photos, and video. Submit your own dance media (text, photo, or video) to contribute to the evolving global story about dance!
